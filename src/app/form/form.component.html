NBC Universal Policy

The receiver of this equipment is responsible for reading and adhering to the guidelines outlined below:

Hardware

The Receiver’s Business unit is responsible for the full cost of repair or replacement of any or all of the Equipment that is damaged, lost, or stolen from the time equipment is received until it is returned to the TV Production IT Client Services (TVP) Team. If the Equipment is lost, stolen or damaged, the receiver agrees to promptly notify the TVP team at tvp.support@nbcuni.com.

Software

All NBCUniversal computers are delivered with standard configuration and pre-installed software. Certain software components are critical to the secure operation of your computer. NBCUniversal workers must never attempt to disable, modify or uninstall security and management software. In addition, NBCUniversal workers must not create additional “User Accounts” or tamper with the accounts that were pre-configured by the TVP team.

Support and Maintenance

Technical support can be obtained by calling the NBCU Global Service Desk at (818)777-4887 or by contacting the TVP team directly at tvp.support@nbcuni.com.

Return of Equipment and Software

At the end of the season or end of employment with the company, you must return any NBCUniversal-owned computer equipment, communication devices or software. The equipment and software should be returned in good working order with software and related accessories. Arrangements must be made, prior to departure, by contacting the TVP team at tvp.support@nbcuni.com
